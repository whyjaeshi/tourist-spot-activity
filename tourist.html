Philippines

The country is known for having its rich biodiversity as its main tourist attraction

Why Philippine is the best tourist spot?

The Philippines is also home to world-renewed naturan wonders like an underground river and rice terraces. Incredible diving spots rich in biodiversity, unique cuisine, vibrant festivals that showcase its colorful culture.

Why is Philippine calles Pearl of the Orient?

Following through its name, the Philippines is the producer of pearls and the important source of the Golden South Sea Peals. At present, most of the pearl farming activity is found in the island of Palawan.
